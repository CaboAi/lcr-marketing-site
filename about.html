Our Story

Built for Baja, By People Who Live Here

We know the struggle of finding a long-term home in Los Cabos. That's why we built the solution.

Our Mission

We believe finding a long-term home in Los Cabos shouldn't require WhatsApp groups, Facebook stalking, or trusting random posts. We're building the platform that centralizes and professionalizes the entire rental experience.

From verified listings and digital leases to automated payments and maintenance tracking, every feature is designed to bring transparency, security, and ease to renters, landlords, and agents alike.

Our Journey

From idea to platform -- the milestones that define us.

Q3 2024

The Idea is Born

After struggling to find a long-term rental in Cabo, our founder decided the market needed a dedicated, professional platform.

Q1 2025

Development Begins

Team assembled, market research completed, and the first lines of code written. Partnerships formed with local property managers.

Q3 2025

Beta Launch

Invite-only beta with select landlords and renters in Cabo San Lucas. Refining the platform based on real user feedback.

Q1 2026

Public Launch

Full platform launch across Los Cabos with agent network, automated payments, and expansion to San Jose del Cabo and El Tezal.

Los Cabos Rental App ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Platform"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, information collected automatically when you use the Platform, and information from third-party sources.

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and password when you create an account.
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued identification (passport, INE, or driver's license), photographs for identity matching, and proof of address.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, credit or debit card numbers, and billing addresses for payment processing. We do not store complete payment card numbers on our servers.
  • Rental Application Data: Employment history, income verification, rental history, references, and credit authorization.
  • Property Information (Landlords): Property addresses, ownership documentation, photographs, floor plans, and amenity descriptions.
  • Professional Credentials (Agents): Real estate license numbers, brokerage affiliations, professional certifications, and tax identification numbers (RFC).
  • Communications: Messages sent through the Platform, support inquiries, and feedback submissions.

Usage Data Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device model, unique device identifiers, and screen resolution.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, referring URLs, and actions taken on the Platform.
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location derived from your IP address, and precise location data if you grant permission through your device settings (used for property search and mapping features).
  • Interaction Data: Search queries, listing views, saved properties, and engagement patterns with the Platform.

Information from Third Parties

  • Background Check Services: Criminal history, credit reports, and eviction records (with your authorization).
  • Social Login Providers: If you sign in using Google or Apple, we receive your name, email, and profile picture.
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmation, payment status, and fraud detection signals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Platform Operations: To create and manage your account, process rental applications, facilitate communication between users, and execute lease agreements.
  • Payment Processing: To process rent payments, security deposits, agent commissions, and platform fees.
  • Verification & Trust: To verify user identities, validate property ownership, confirm professional credentials, and conduct background checks when authorized.
  • Personalization: To customize search results, recommend properties based on your preferences, and provide location-relevant content.
  • Communication: To send transactional notifications (payment confirmations, application updates), respond to support inquiries, and deliver service announcements.
  • Safety & Security: To detect and prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, protect against unauthorized access, and maintain Platform integrity.
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand usage patterns, measure Platform performance, identify bugs, and improve our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal processes, and fulfill regulatory obligations under Mexican law.
  • Marketing (with consent): To send promotional content about new features, market updates, and rental opportunities in Los Cabos. You can opt out at any time.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Between Users: Landlords can see Renter application information. Renters can see Landlord contact details for listed properties. Agents receive relevant information to facilitate transactions they are managing.
  • Service Providers: We share information with trusted third-party service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as payment processing, identity verification, cloud hosting, email delivery, and analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify affected users before their information is subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information for purposes not described in this policy with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment processing through certified third-party providers
  • Two-factor authentication available for all user accounts
  • Automated threat detection and monitoring systems

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will promptly notify affected users in the event of a data breach in accordance with applicable Mexican data protection laws (Ley Federal de Proteccion de Datos Personales en Posesion de los Particulares).

5. Your Rights

Under Mexican data protection law (LFPDPPP) and international best practices, you have the following rights regarding your personal information (known as ARCO rights in Mexico):

  • Access (Acceso): You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and obtain a copy of that information.
  • Rectification (Rectificacion): You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Cancellation (Cancelacion): You have the right to request deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opposition (Oposicion): You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for specific purposes, such as marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for the processing of your personal data at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing performed prior to withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@loscabosrentalapp.com. We will respond to verified requests within twenty (20) business days, as required by Mexican law. We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing your request.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Platform. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core Platform functionality, such as maintaining your login session and processing payments. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, language selection, and search settings to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Platform, which pages are most visited, and where users encounter errors. We use this data to improve Platform performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. These are only activated with your explicit consent.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features of the Platform from functioning properly.

We also use web beacons (pixel tags) in emails to track open rates and click-through rates, helping us improve our communications. You can disable image loading in your email client to prevent web beacon tracking.

7. Third-Party Services

The Platform integrates with the following categories of third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and local Mexican payment providers for secure transaction processing.
  • Identity Verification: Third-party providers for document verification, facial recognition matching, and background screening.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting and data storage services provided by established cloud computing platforms.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics and similar tools for understanding Platform usage patterns.
  • Communication: Email delivery services, SMS notification providers, and in-app messaging platforms.
  • Mapping: Google Maps for property location display and neighborhood exploration features.

We carefully select third-party partners that maintain adequate data protection standards. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specific retention periods include:

  • Active Accounts: Personal data is retained for the duration of your account activity plus two (2) years after account closure.
  • Transaction Records: Payment and rental transaction data is retained for five (5) years to comply with Mexican tax and accounting regulations.
  • Identity Verification Documents: Copies of identity documents are retained for three (3) years after account closure, as required by anti-money laundering regulations.
  • Communication Logs: Messages and support tickets are retained for two (2) years from the date of the communication.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for statistical and research purposes.

When personal data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized so that it can no longer be associated with you. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us, subject to legal retention obligations.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our systems.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at privacy@loscabosrentalapp.com so we can take appropriate action.

10. International Data Transfers

Los Cabos Rental App primarily processes and stores data within Mexico. However, some of our third-party service providers may process data in other jurisdictions, including the United States and the European Economic Area.

When personal data is transferred outside of Mexico, we ensure that adequate safeguards are in place through:

  • Contractual clauses requiring the recipient to maintain equivalent levels of data protection
  • Verification that the receiving jurisdiction provides adequate data protection standards
  • Technical measures such as encryption to protect data during transfer

By using the Platform, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your personal data to jurisdictions outside of Mexico where our service providers operate, subject to the protections described above.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify registered users via email at least fifteen (15) days before changes take effect
  • Display a prominent notice on the Platform

Your continued use of the Platform after the effective date of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information about our privacy practices.

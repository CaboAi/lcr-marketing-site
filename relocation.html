What We Help With

Relocating to a new country can feel overwhelming. We have broken it down into manageable steps and handle each one for you.

home_pin

Home Finding

Curated property selection based on your needs, budget, and lifestyle preferences. We pre-screen listings so you only see homes that truly fit.

gavel

Visa & Legal

Immigration guidance and rental law basics. We connect you with trusted attorneys for temporary and permanent residency visas and lease review.

school

School Search

International school recommendations for families. We provide tours, enrollment guidance, and honest reviews of schools from other expat parents.

electrical_services

Utility Setup

CFE electricity, high-speed internet, water, and gas. We handle the paperwork, appointments, and installations so everything is ready on move-in day.

tour

Neighborhood Tours

Guided visits to Pedregal, El Tezal, The Corridor, and more. Experience each neighborhood firsthand before committing to a location.

diversity_3

Community Introduction

Connect with expat groups, local Facebook communities, sports leagues, and social clubs. We make sure you never feel alone in your new home.
